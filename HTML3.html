Easy Turkey Crown Ingredients

Cooking Time: Roughly 1 hour 40 mins
  1. 50g of Softened Butter
  2. 2 kg of Turkey Crown to the Bone
  3. 1 tsp of Chinese Five Spice or Pinch of Ground Cloves
For the Glaze
  • 4 tbsp of Honey
  • 1 tbsp of Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp of Red Wine Vinegar
After obtaining the ingredients listed above, it is now time to start cooking!

How to Cook the Easy Turkey Crown

Before we start actually cooking, we must heat oven to 190C/170C and leave fan or gas at 5. Next we must smear the butter all over the turkey crown and season all over with salt and half the five spice. Once you are done seasoning the turkey, put in a roasting tin, skin-side up, and roast for 30 mins. While we wait for the crown to finish roasting, we can mix all the glaze ingredients in a bowl with the rest of the five spice.

Once we have finished the glase, we can remove the crown from the oven and brush the skin handsomely with half the glaze. Continue to roast for another hour, glazing twice more, until cooked all the way through and the glaze is sticky and caramelised. Leave to rest for at least 20 mins before carving. Save the juices from the tin to make gravy. You can always add some of your own touches to make this better.
